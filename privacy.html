SMS Text Messaging Privacy Policy

Pop-A-Lock of Northern New Jersey · Effective July 7, 2026

Pop-A-Lock of Northern New Jersey, a business operated by Evergreen Systems, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"), operates an internal text-messaging program used to notify our technicians and managers about job dispatches during their shifts. This policy explains what information the program uses, how we use it, and your choices. It applies only to our SMS/text messaging program.

The messaging program

Our dispatch system sends operational text messages and automated voice calls to on-shift technicians and their managers — for example, an alert that a new service call has been assigned to a technician, and follow-up "wake-up" reminders that escalate to a manager if a dispatch has not been acknowledged. These are operational, work-related messages. We do not use this program to send marketing or promotional messages.

Information we use

  • The mobile phone numbers of our technicians, managers, and staff, provided by them as part of their work roster.
  • Basic message activity needed to operate and troubleshoot the program (for example, whether a dispatch alert was delivered or acknowledged).

How we do NOT share your information

No mobile information — including mobile phone numbers and SMS opt-in or consent data — will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Text-messaging opt-in and consent data is not shared with, sold, rented, or otherwise provided to any third parties. The only parties that ever handle it are the messaging-service providers (for example, our SMS and voice carrier) that transmit the messages on our behalf, acting purely as the delivery mechanism — not as data recipients — and only to the extent required to send the messages you have agreed to receive. Mobile information is used solely to operate this internal dispatch messaging program.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of data sharing exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties, excluding aggregators and providers of the Text Message services.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on shift schedules and dispatch activity. A technician may receive messages when a job is assigned to them and when a dispatch is escalated; on a busy shift this may be several messages, and on a quiet shift, none.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply. Any charges are billed by your mobile carrier under your own plan; we do not control or reimburse those charges.

Opting out and getting help

  • Reply STOP to any message to opt out and stop receiving texts at any time. You may receive a final confirmation that you have been unsubscribed.
  • Reply HELP for assistance, or contact us using the details below.

How consent is obtained

Recipients are technicians and managers of Pop-A-Lock of Northern New Jersey, and these messages are sent to company-issued or technician-provided mobile numbers enrolled in our dispatch roster. Each recipient gives consent to receive these operational dispatch messages in one of two ways:

  • By submitting our opt-in form at popalocknj.com/optin.html, where they enter their mobile number and check a box agreeing to receive dispatch text alerts; or
  • By texting START to our dispatch number (+1 862 375 4731).

Consent to receive these messages is not a condition of purchase. A recipient may withdraw consent at any time by replying STOP.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always posted at this page, with the effective date shown above.
