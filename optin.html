Dispatch Text Alerts — Opt In

Pop-A-Lock of Northern New Jersey · for our technicians & managers

This page is for Pop-A-Lock of Northern New Jersey field technicians and managers to sign up for our internal dispatch text-alert program. When you are issued a company phone or enroll your work mobile number, use this form to give your consent to receive operational dispatch texts on that number.

What you are signing up for

  • Brief operational alerts when a service call is assigned to you (for example, “Wake up Mike — new call on the board in Newark. Pulsar.”).
  • Follow-up “wake-up” reminders if a dispatch has not been acknowledged, which may escalate to your manager.
  • These are work-related messages only. We never send marketing or promotional texts through this program.

Consent is not a condition of purchase. Your number is used only to operate this dispatch program and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Prefer to opt in by text?

You can also opt in from your phone: text START to +1 862 375 4731. Reply STOP to any message to opt out, or HELP for help.

Pop-A-Lock of Northern New Jersey is operated by Evergreen Systems, Inc. This page and our SMS policies are provided to meet mobile carrier (CTIA) and messaging-provider requirements for application-to-person (A2P) messaging.
