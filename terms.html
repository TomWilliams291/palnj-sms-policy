Program description

Our dispatch system sends operational text messages, and related automated voice calls, to on-shift technicians and their managers — for example, an alert that a new service call has been assigned to a technician, and follow-up "wake-up" reminders that escalate to a manager if a dispatch has not been acknowledged. These are operational, work-related messages. This program is not used for marketing or promotional messages.

Who receives messages and how they opt in

Recipients are technicians and managers of Pop-A-Lock of Northern New Jersey, and messages are sent to company-issued or technician-provided mobile numbers enrolled in our dispatch roster. Each recipient gives consent to receive these operational dispatch messages in one of two ways:

  • By submitting our opt-in form at popalocknj.com/optin.html, where they enter their mobile number and check a box agreeing to receive dispatch text alerts; or
  • By texting START to our dispatch number (+1 862 375 4731).

Consent to receive these messages is not a condition of purchase.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on shift schedules and dispatch activity. A technician may receive messages when a job is assigned to them and when a dispatch is escalated; on a busy shift this may be several messages, and on a quiet shift, none.

Opting out (STOP) and help (HELP)
